Information about MOSAIC and the RISE team.

Application Features

Documents

Browse, search, and review documents with AI-assisted batch processing.

Tags

Group and enrich named entities from transcriptions with authority IDs.

Metadata

Manage and review custom metadata fields for documents and pages.

Dictionaries

Build controlled vocabularies and enrich entries with GND, Wikidata, and GeoNames.

Collections

Create and review curated collections of text passages with AI analysis.

Import/Export

Configure exports to various formats and publish to Zenodo repositories.

Project Settings

Configure AI providers, workflows, credentials, and user permissions.

AI Integration

Connect multiple AI providers (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek) for content analysis.

This service is hosted and developed by the RISE team at the University of Basel.

You can learn more about our work and access the source code on our GitHub repository.
